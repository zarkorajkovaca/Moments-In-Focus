About the Gallery

My name is Rajkovača Žarko. I am an professional photographer located in Serbia, and I am doing every type of photography.

Moments in Focus is a professional website specializing in photography services, offering top-quality and creative solutions for all types of events and needs.

Our services include wedding photography, portraits, family and corporate photos, as well as artistic projects.

The website is user-friendly and easy to navigate, featuring an extensive portfolio, detailed service information, and an online booking option. Whether you’re looking for elegant memories or professional visuals, the Moments in Focus team is here to turn your moments into lasting stories.

This gallery features a collection of photos from various events and locations. The aim is to showcase the beauty of everyday moments captured through the lens.

The gallery is curated by an amateur photographer with a passion for exploring the world and capturing its essence.



I am currently located in Novi Sad and Sombor.
